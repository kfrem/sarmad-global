Our Story and Credentials

Combining the rigorous compliance standards of a chartered accounting practice with the efficiency of modern cloud software and automated systems.

Speak With Our Principal

A Deliberate Dual Approach

Sarmad Global was established to bridge a clear gap in the UK professional services market. Many businesses require experienced, technical chartered accountancy but struggle to find firms that understand modern digital workflows. Conversely, many accounting practices want to modernise and automate their administrative pipelines but lack the bespoke software capabilities to do so in-house.

We represent over 30 years of cumulative practice experience across statutory accounting, direct corporate taxation, practice workflow automation, and custom web development. This unique dual background allows us to protect your compliance securely while engineering highly efficient operational systems.
