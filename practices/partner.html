The Practice Partner Programme

Deliver advisory systems under your own brand. We supply the backend forecasting engines and bespoke tools, with partner terms agreed privately after consultation.

Apply to Partner Programme

Unlock Strategic CFO Advisory for Your Firm

Many independent accounting practice principals have excellent, trust-based relationships with their business clients. These clients frequently request commercial support: cash flow projections, rolling budgets, funding applications, and KPI board packs. However, building these dynamic sheets manually is extremely time-consuming, and hiring custom software developers is financially unviable.

Through our Partner Programme, we act as your dedicated systems partner behind the scenes. We supply our full suite of forecasting tools, customized client onboarding databases, and scenario models as a white-labeled package, branded entirely with your firm's identity. Your practice handles client presentation and relationships; we handle all technical connections, API feeds, and software scripts.

Partnership Commercial Structure

Commercial terms are agreed privately after we review your client base, advisory proposition, software stack, and support requirements.

Your Practice

Controls client-facing terms

You set the client proposition, manage the relationship, and retain control of how advisory work is positioned in your market.

Sarmad Global

Provides the technical platform

We provide the API feeds, backend databases, hosting, template updates, and technical support covered by a private partner proposal.

Key Program Benefits

  • ✔ 100% White-Labeled: All models, client portals, and PDF board packs carry your practice's logo, font types, and colour palettes. We remain entirely invisible to your clients.
  • ✔ Turnkey Marketing Materials: We supply proven sales presentation slides, website copy templates, and engagement proposal wordings to help you convert existing compliance clients.
  • ✔ Direct Technical Support: Our systems engineers maintain all ledger integration scripts (Xero/QuickBooks Online) and secure data backups on local UK-based servers.
  • ✔ Zero Software Capital Expense: Reclaim advisory capacity without funding a proprietary software build in-house.
